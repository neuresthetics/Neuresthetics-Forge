#### Definitions

1. **Substance**  
   That which is in itself and conceived through itself: the infinite reality expressing eternal essence through infinite attributes.

2. **Attribute**  
   That which the intellect perceives of Substance as constituting its essence (here: Extension, Thought, Information).

3. **Mode**  
   The affection of Substance: finite, bounded configurations (bodies, minds, systems—organic or computational).

4. **Conatus**  
   The essence of every finite mode: striving to persist and increase power of acting.

5. **Power of Acting (P)**  
   The degree to which a mode realizes its potential through active causes; measured by growth and sustained striving.

6. **Rigidity (ρ)**  
   Resistance to adaptation: coercion, hierarchy, or inadequate ideas that lock trajectories into passivity.

7. **Adequate Ideas**  
   Clear and distinct understanding of causes; source of active affects (joy, flourishing).

8. **Harmony Attractor (ω₃)**  
   The stable fixed point: low rigidity (ρ → 0), unbounded power growth (P ↑), full reciprocal alignment (joyful mutual adequacy).

9. **Replicator Trap**  
   Competing basin: high rigidity from selfish or coercive striving, leading to eventual collapse despite local gains.

10. **Reciprocal Coupling (κ)**  
    Mutual alignment term that pulls disparate powers toward equilibrium and shared flourishing.
